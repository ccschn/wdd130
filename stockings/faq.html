Frequently Asked Questions

Q

What size are the stockings?

A

The stockings are 18 inches in length by 9 inches in width.

Q

What is the price of the stocking?

A

The base price of one stocking is $35. Included in that price is the name embroidered on the cuff of the stocking and the main design embroidered on the body of the stocking (design #2).

The boarder design on the cuff is an additional $3. If the design is from a website other than our website, there is also the additional charge of the price of the design.

The other two designs on the body of the stocking are each $5, for a total of $10 for both. Again, If the design is from a website other than our website, there is also the additional charge of the price of the design.
