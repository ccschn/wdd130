Colors

On this page we have provided pictures of stocking color options. We have a bright Christmas red, a darker Christmas red, a beautiful royal blue, a navy blue, and a Christmas green. There is also a white fabric for the cuff. We are able to switch the fabric to make the body of the stocking white and the cuff one of the colored fabrics. Our Christmas stocking are designed to be personal creations and as such, you are able to pick one fabric color for all of the stockings in your order, each stocking is able to be a different color, or any combination of colors.

Embroidery Designs

This page also provides embroidery designs that we own and have used in the Christmas stockings we have created. We also provide links to other websites that provide embroidery designs for purchase. In your email, the designs you are choosing from our website can be included just via the design name. If you are wanting a design from a different website, please provide the link to the exact design to ensure we put the correct design on your stocking. The price of the embroidery design from a different website will be added to the cost of the order.
