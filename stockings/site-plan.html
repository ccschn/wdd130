Overview

Purpose

I am a seamstress. I work mainly with Christmas stockings. My company makes individualized stockings for everyone. You can choose your own stocking colors (doesn’t have to just be red) and pick from all sorts of different designs to be embroidered onto the stocking to make them last and be unique. Have you ever pulled out your Christmas stockings and wished they were more personalized and special, not quite so generic, and tacky? I do that for families. I keep the magic of Christmas alive year after year, with completely customizable elegant stockings. Stockings for every style of the season.

Audience

The website is targeted to adults, mainly those in their 20s to late 50s who have families or a love of Christmas decorating. By offering a wide range of fabric colors and embroidery patterns, people can design their perfect Christmas stocking. We are targeting people who are middle class and higher who want Christmas decorations that have meaning and purpose behind them, not just a generic stocking bought at a grocery store. Our website will mainly be accessed from a laptop or desktop computer.

Below is a diagram of a Christmas stocking. This is the design we recommend. If you are wanting a different design of patterns on your stocking, you may email a detailed description of an alternative design along with an image or link to the design. FAQ

What size are the stockings? The stockings are 18 inches in length by 9 inches in width. What needs to be provided in the emailed order? Here is an email template for an order: Number of stockings: Stocking #1: Color of cuff: Color of body: Name on stocking cuff: Cuff boarder: Design #1: Design #2: Design #3: Stocking #2: Color of cuff: Color of body: Name on stocking cuff: Cuff boarder: Design #1: Design #2: Design #3: Continue adding stockings until you have added each stocking in your order. What is the price of the stocking? The base price of one stocking is $35. Included in that price is the name embroidered on the cuff of the stocking and the main design embroidered on the body of the stocking (design #2). The boarder design on the cuff is an additional $3. If the design is from a website other than our website, there is also the additional charge of the price of the design. The other two designs on the body of the stocking are each $5, for a total of $10 for both. Again, If the design is from a website other than our website, there is also the additional charge of the price of the design.
